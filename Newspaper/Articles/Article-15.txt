                   <p><b>New Delhi</b>: After the Aam Aadmi party minister taunted Mehbooba Mufti,Chief Minister of Jammu and Kashmir, for not accepting Hizbul Mujahiddin commander Burhan Wani as a terrorist, the minister retorted by commenting on the shameful 'rape capital' tag that the city has earned through its numerous number of brutal rape cases.</p>
<p>On Tuesday(4-10-2016), at the launch function of the Bharat International Travel Bazaar(BITB), Kapil Sharma(Delhi tourism minister) questioned Mufti if she accepts Burhan Wani as terrorist or not and how can she expect tourism to increase when terrorists are treated as tourists in Kashmir. Mufti in the month of July had said that she would have given Burhan "a chance" had she known he was trapped in an encounter. However, the minister in her reposite said that no tourists were ever harmed in Kashmir and that tolerance is their biggest strength as they are followers  of Gandhi. She further said that girls are the safest in Kashmir and can roam around in Shikaras and houseboats fearlessly unlike Delhi where they can get raped in moving cars.</p>
<p>She further upheld Kashmir tourism by saying that we have the people can build our confidence by visiting our state to show that they trust us. She appealed to people to come and visit and invest as Kashmir needs them.</p>
